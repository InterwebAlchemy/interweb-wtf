## Tracking Parameters

[Tracking parameters](https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/6277564) are a way for websites to track how users arrived at or shared their content.

Interweb.WTF does not use tracking parameters to track users and actively removes tracking parameters from URLs when creating [WTF Links](/docs/concepts/wtf-links) or [expanding shortlinks](/docs/expand).

We also provide a free [URL Cleaner](/docs/clean) that removes tracking parameters from any URL.
